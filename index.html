"Chipolte BBQ Sauce"

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup molasses
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 4 tablespoons minced chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 4 tablespoons distilled sinegar
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • Dash of salt

Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Add the garlic and onions and cook for 5 minutes. Reduce the heat to low. Add the ketchup, molasses, brown sugar, chipolte peppers, vinegar, worcestershire sauce and salt. Allow to simmer for 20 minutes.
